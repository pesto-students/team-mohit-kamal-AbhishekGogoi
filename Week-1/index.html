Dibrugarh, India

Location

Dibrugarh is an industrial city in Upper Assam with sprawling tea gardens. It is located 435 kms East from the state capital of Dispur.

About

Weather

Dibrugarh has a humid subtropical climate with extremely wet summers and relatively dry winters.

Population

The current estimate population of Dibrugarh city in 2023 is 191,000 , while Dibrugarh metro population is estimated at 212,000.

Sports

Cricket and Football are two of the popular sports in Dibrugarh.
